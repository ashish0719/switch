## About The Project

Switch is designed to be your all-in-one platform for career acceleration. Whether you are polishing your resume or looking for curated study materials, we've got you covered.

### What We Have Now
*   **Resume Optimization**: Our core feature. Upload your resume and a job description, and our AI will help you tailor your application to stand out.
*   **Notes & Resources**: A dedicated section for sharing knowledge. Admins can upload important PDFs and notes, which are securely stored on Cloudflare R2 and easily accessible to everyone via our custom PDF viewer.
*   **Secure Authentication**: A robust login and registration system ensuring your data stays private.

### Coming Soon
We are constantly building! Here is what's on the way:
*   **Jobs**: A specialized job board to find opportunities that match your improved resume.
*   **Modules**: Structured learning paths and modules to upskill yourself.

## Tech Stack
*   **Frontend**: React, Vite, Tailwind CSS
*   **Backend**: Node.js, Express
*   **Storage**: MongoDB (Data), Cloudflare R2 (Files)
*   **AI**: Powered by Gemini
